Breakpoint contract

Open this page in DevTools and resize to see how layouts react. The current viewport tier is auto-detected below.

Tier Range Behaviour
Mobile ≤ 767px Burger header, 1-col stacks, mobile bento, no topbar at ≤640.
Tablet 768 – 1023px Sticky header, 2-col reviews, "Alle" pill in category strip.
Desktop ≥ 1024px Full nav strip, multi-col grids, Kategorien pill on scroll.
